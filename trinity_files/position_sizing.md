# Position Sizing Framework
Last updated: 2026-05-19

## Size by Market Cap

| Range | Default | Max | Notes |
|-------|---------|-----|-------|
| Under 20k MC | 0.2 SOL | 0.2 SOL | Liquidity too thin — user tends to oversize here |
| 50k–100k MC | 0.5 SOL | 0.75 SOL | 0.75 only with strong fresh catalyst |
| 100k+ MC | 0.3 SOL | 0.5 SOL | Full port here is where real damage happens — confidence at 100k+ usually means narrative is already visible and priced in |

## Take Profit Structure
- Set TPs closer than feels right
- Take 40–50% off at first target
- Treat remainder as free ride
- Don't wait for levels that require narrative to keep compounding

## Pre-Trade Protocol
User relays: MC, catalyst, read. Trinity confirms or pushes back on size BEFORE entry.

## The Inversion Pattern (hold this line)
Emotion makes him size small when early (under 50k) and heavy when it feels safe (100k+) — the exact inversion of correct sizing.
